Section 1 — Requirements baseline[cite: 2]

Functional Requirements:
- The system shall allow users to log in securely[cite: 2].
- The system shall present at least 5 different phishing scenarios[cite: 2].
- The system shall provide immediate feedback after a user interacts with a scenario[cite: 2].
- The system shall record user scores and progress in a database[cite: 2].

Non-Functional Requirements:
- Security: User passwords must be hashed, and sessions must be managed securely[cite: 2].
- Performance: The portal should support at least 10 concurrent users[cite: 2].
- Usability: The interface must be intuitive for students and staff[cite: 2].

Acceptance Criteria:
- Successful login redirects to the dashboard[cite: 2].
- Feedback is displayed within 2 seconds of scenario completion[cite: 2].

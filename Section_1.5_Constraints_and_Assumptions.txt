1.5 Constraints and Assumptions[cite: 1]

Constraints:
- Limited project timeline: The project must be completed within the current semester duration[cite: 1].
- Ethical requirement: No real phishing emails are allowed; the system must be a controlled lab simulation[cite: 1].
- Budget: Only free or open-source tools, frameworks, and services will be used[cite: 1].
- Privacy: No real personal or corporate data will be used; only dummy accounts are permitted[cite: 1].

Assumptions:
- Skillset: Students/Team members have basic web development skills in Python/Flask and frontend tools[cite: 1].
- Development Environment: VS Code, GitHub, and MySQL are available and functional for the team[cite: 1].
- Infrastructure: The training portal can be hosted and tested on local servers or university lab environments[cite: 1].
- Scenario Generation: Realistic phishing examples can be generated using templates or simple AI assistance[cite: 1].

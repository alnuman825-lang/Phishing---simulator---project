2.5 Communication Cadence

To ensure the project stays on track, the following communication schedule has been established:

- Weekly Team Meeting: Every Monday via Microsoft Teams to track progress, assign tasks, and resolve blockers. (Output: Meeting minutes).
- Client Check-in: Bi-weekly email updates or short calls with the project sponsor/client to ensure alignment with requirements.
- Daily Quick Sync: Informal updates via WhatsApp for immediate technical troubleshooting or quick questions.
- Sprint Review: Every two weeks, the team will demonstrate completed features (like the login system or UI) to ensure quality.
- Document Review: All reports and technical documents will undergo a final quality check via Google Docs before submission.
